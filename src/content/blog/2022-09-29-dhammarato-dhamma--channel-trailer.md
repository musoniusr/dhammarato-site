**Speaker A:** Welcome to the Dhammarato Dharma YouTube channel. On this channel are recorded conversations between me and my friends. Western Buddhist teaching has become something for a mass audience, but the supramundane Dharma of the Buddha is best taught in a private setting where the spiritual friends can assist the students in correct understanding by way of questions. Questions and answers, sutta comparisons, and instructions on correct practice that will lead to your joy, well-being, and happiness for a long time. This YouTube project has been started with the encouragement and full knowledge of Bhikkhu Achan Po, the Abbot Emeritus of Wat Suwan Mokkh in Chaiya, Thailand. Anagami, home of Bhikkhu Buddhadasa. A little bit about me. I started off as a child, was raised in the deep racist South, became a musician, became an engineer, became a psychologist, became a magician, became a traveler in Asia, became a master magician, became a student to Bhikkhu Buddhadasa, became a Buddhist monk, and then stopped becoming and started having fun, started having a ball. Now nothing much, just sharing the joy. We have Sangha calls on Skype 3 times a week, and have an active community of Dharma friends on Discord. All are welcome to join. The links are in the description of this video. You can also listen to these videos on any of your favorite podcast apps. If you're not sure where to start, check out the introduction series with Alex and enjoy.
