### **1. The Framework: Seven Methods to Eradicate the "Taints" (ƒÄsavas)**
The sutta outlines seven ways to remove mental defilements:
*   **1. By Seeing (Knowledge):** The most profound method. It involves paying **wise attention to the Four Noble Truths**‚Äîseeing suffering, its cause, its end, and the path. *   **3. By Using (the Requisites):** Using the four monastic requisites (food, clothing, shelter, medicine) **wisely**‚Äîfor nourishment and support, not for pleasure or indulgence.
