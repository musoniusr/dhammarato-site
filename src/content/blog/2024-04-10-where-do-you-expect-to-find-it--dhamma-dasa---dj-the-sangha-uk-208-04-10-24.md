*   **Remember:** To check in with your present state of mind.
*   **Look:** Honestly assess if there is dissatisfaction, worry, or craving.
*   **Change:** Make a wholesome adjustment in thought, speech, or action to "gladden the mind" right here and now. This often involves nurturing self-talk (e.g., "Everything is okay right now"), coming back to the breath, or relaxing the body.
*   **Rinse and Repeat:** This is not a one-time fix but a habit to build throughout the day.
