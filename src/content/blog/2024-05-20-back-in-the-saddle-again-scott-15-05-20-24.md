### Summary

### **Core Teaching: You Shape Your Destiny Through Present Actions**

*   **The Power of Choice:** Unlike fatalistic religious views, the Buddha's teaching emphasizes you have a **choice**. By changing your thoughts, you can change your actions, reform habits, and create a new destiny. This is true "repentance"‚Äîturning around and going a new direction.
