### Summary

This Dhamma talk, led by Dhammarato with a group of Sangha friends, centers on the core insight that **suffering (Dukkha) arises from wanting what we don't have**‚Äîand that the solution is not to fight desire with more desire, but to see it clearly and cultivate inner satisfaction.
