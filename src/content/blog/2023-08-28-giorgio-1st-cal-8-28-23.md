4.  **The Practical Method: The Noble Eightfold Path as a Feedback Loop:**
    *   **Right View (Pa√±√±a)** is not a fixed belief but an **action**: **"right viewing."** It means constantly investigating, looking, and checking the mind.
    *   **Sati (Mindfulness)** is the **ability to remember** to wake up from mental stories and come back to the present moment to investigate.
    *   **Right Effort** is the **change mechanism**: When you remember to look (sati), you use discernment to see if a thought is **wholesome** (leading to peace, here-and-now) or **unwholesome** (leading to harm, about past/future/elsewhere).
    *   **You change unwholesome thoughts to wholesome ones.** You don't try to stop the mind (like trying to stop a giant ship); you **steer** it. For example, change a thought of resentment toward someone into respect or simply let it go for a thought of present-moment contentment.
